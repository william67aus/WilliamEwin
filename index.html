William Ewin

My name is William Ewin, I am a university student studying a Bachelor of Design (Digital Media) at RMIT University. My work tends to revolve around my hometown and the environment in which I grew up in. Due to living in the city, I realised the many things that I took for granted, and I utilise these aspects in my work, highlighting the places that I miss most. Through my future work, I hope to express more areas of the coastal town, showcasing how much of a beautiful place it is.

Project 2:

Elements of Nature

My idea behind the icons was to portray the natural elements that are in our environment and produce icons that relate to each one. My initial prompt was nature, I wanted to showcase the different sides of that, thinking of weather and time. I used monographic colours for each icon having a different overall colour for each, showcasing the colours that I felt were most appropriate for each element. I tried to pay close attention to the initial thought of 'what would this icon sound like. Through this I found some odd sounds to portray some icons.

Project 3:

Two Worlds

Two Worlds is a concept revolving around my move from a small coastal town to the city of Melbourne. I wanted to portray key features of my hometown to help showcase where I grew up and provide the player with an experience that I used to have almost daily. Through the use of sound, I was able to create two contrasting environments and produce an atmosphere that I wanted the players to experience. I wanted to achieve a game with two separate environments but enticed the player to one of them specifically.
